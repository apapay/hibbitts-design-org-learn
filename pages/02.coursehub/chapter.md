# Open Course Hub

[Grav Open Course Hub](https://github.com/hibbitts-design/grav-skeleton-course-hub) with [Git Sync](https://github.com/trilbymedia/grav-plugin-git-sync) is designed to empower tech-savvy instructors with an open, collaborative and flexible environment not present in their current Learning Management System (LMS).

Explore a sample Open Course Hub site at: <br> [demo.hibbittsdesign.org/grav-course-hub](http://demo.hibbittsdesign.org/grav-course-hub-bootstrap/)
